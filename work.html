    Artifical Intelligence

    Artificial intelligence is intelligence exhibited by machines, rather than humans or other animals (natural intelligence). In computer science, the field of AI research defines itself as the study of "intelligent agents": any device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of success at a predefined goal.
